Featured Dishes

Delve into our signature dishes, each prepared with passion, authenticity, and love.

Full Thali

A grand feast, featuring a variety of delicacies that bring together the perfect balance of spices and flavors. A true indulgence.

Samosa

Crisp golden pockets filled with spiced potatoes and peas. Served with tangy tamarind chutney for that perfect crunch.

Chapati

Soft and fluffy flatbreads, perfect to pair with any curry or dish. Made fresh to order for an authentic experience.

Dori Chapati

A twist on the classic chapati, rolled into a coiled shape and cooked to perfection, offering a delightful texture with every bite.

Loki Sabji

A comforting dish made with bottle gourd and aromatic spices, offering a light yet satisfying vegetarian option.

Dal Dhokali

A hearty lentil-based curry, paired with soft, savory wheat dumplings. A rustic dish full of flavor and tradition.

Sev Tamato

A flavorful tomato curry, topped with crispy sev (fried chickpea noodles) for a delightful combination of textures and flavors.

Kaddu Sabji

A delightful pumpkin curry, infused with aromatic spices that bring out its natural sweetness and savory goodness.
